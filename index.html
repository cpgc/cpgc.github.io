The Communist Party of Glendowie

How many of these people do you recognise? You’ll probably recognise Pablo Picasso, up in the top left – Martin Luther King Jr. and Albert Einstein, and probably Nelson Mandela too. You might also notice Bertrand Russell – a famous mathematician and logician – Malala Yousafzai, the youngest-ever Nobel Prize laureate and a Pakistani human rights activist, and Xi Jinping, the President of China, are up there too. Even we didn’t recognise the two people on the bottom right – Leo Tolstoy, the author of War and Peace, and H. G Wells. Oh, and Richard Liu, a Chinese internet billionaire. He's the one with the mic. Ok, trivia quiz. How many of these people either were, or are, socialists?
